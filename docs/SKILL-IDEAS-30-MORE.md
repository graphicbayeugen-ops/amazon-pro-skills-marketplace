## A. Financial Recovery and Reconciliation

**1. amz-reimbursement-audit** *(10/10)*
Walks the seller through Amazon's post-March-2025 reimbursement policy (manufacturing-
cost basis) and outputs a claim packet per case (lost, damaged, customer return,
warehouse damage) with the required cost documentation.
- Pain: 2025 policy slashed payouts up to 60% unless sellers submit their own cost
  basis. 60-day claim windows now apply.
- Framework: case-type decision tree → evidence checklist → templated claim narrative.

**2. amz-cash-flow-forecaster-dd7** *(9/10)*
Models cash flow under DD+7 (Delivery-Date-Based Reserve) given sales velocity,
FBA/FBM mix, seasonality, and outputs working-capital buffer recommendations.
- Pain: DD+7 takes effect March 2026 in the US, Sep 2025 in EU. sellers need
  significant extra working capital.
- Framework: payout-cycle calculator + stress-test scenarios by month.

**3. amz-chargeback-defense** *(9/10)*
Pastes a chargeback or A-to-Z notification, generates the rep packet with evidence
checklist (carrier GPS, signature, messaging history) and a 7-day-window response.
- Pain: 20-40% chargeback dispute win rate, $20 fee even if you win, hits ODR.
- Framework: claim-type matcher → evidence rubric → response narrative.

**4. amz-fee-audit** *(8/10)*
Audits a Payments report CSV for incorrect fee charges (wrong dimensional tier,
missing referral fee credits on refunds, duplicate FBA fees) and produces a SAFE-T
case template.
- Pain: Dimensional-tier misclassification is the number-one hidden fee leak after
  the 2024 size-tier reshuffle.
- Framework: fee-anomaly detection rules + case template per anomaly.

**5. amz-1099k-reconciliation** *(8/10)*
Reconciles Amazon's 1099-K against actual deposits, fees, and refunds for tax filing.
Explains the gap between gross sales and what actually hit the bank.
- Pain: 1099-K excludes refunds and fees, leading sellers to overpay tax or
  under-report.
- Framework: gross-to-net reconciliation worksheet + Schedule C mapping.

## B. Account Health and Compliance Defense

**6. amz-account-health-triage** *(9/10)*
Given a screenshot or paste of the AHR dashboard, identifies which violations pull
the rating down, prioritizes by point weight, and outputs a remediation order.
- Pain: AHR fluctuating green-to-red unpredictably. expanded 2025 visibility.
- Framework: violation-impact rubric + remediation sequence.

**7. amz-ipi-recovery-plan** *(9/10)*
Diagnoses why IPI dropped (excess, sell-through, stranded, in-stock) and outputs a
30-60-90 day plan to lift it.
- Pain: IPI dropping 30-100 points overnight. threshold tightened in mid-2025.
- Framework: 4-lever diagnostic + weekly action checklist.

**8. amz-hijacker-removal** *(9/10)*
Step-by-step takedown sequence for a listing hijacker: test-buy script, evidence
package, Brand Registry violation form, cease-and-desist template, escalation to
CCU (Counterfeit Crimes Unit).
- Pain: Hijacking remains a top forum complaint in 2025.
- Framework: graduated-response playbook with templates per stage.

**9. amz-listing-suppression-recovery** *(9/10)*
Given a suppressed or blocked listing reason code, produces the fix path (image
policy, restricted phrase, dimension mismatch) and a reinstatement message.
- Pain: 'Arbitrary' suppression for high pricing, missing fields, or silent category
  re-nodes.
- Framework: reason-code lookup → fix script.

**10. amz-category-renoding-fix** *(7/10)*
Detects when Amazon silently moved your catalog to wrong category nodes (causing 60%
sales drops) and generates the contributor-portal correction case.
- Pain: 2025 forum issue. sellers reporting catalog-wide silent re-categorization.
- Framework: node-audit checklist + case wording.

**11. amz-buyer-abuse-defense** *(9/10)*
Identifies repeat-return-abuse buyers and INR/materially-different fraud patterns
from order data, and outputs the case for Amazon to refund and ban the buyer.
- Pain: Repeated buyer-fraud loops. top recurring 2025 forum complaint.
- Framework: abuse-pattern rubric → escalation case packet.

## C. AI Search and Modern SEO

**12. amz-rufus-optimization** *(10/10)*
Rewrites a listing for Rufus/COSMO: question-answering bullets, attributes-section
completeness, structured product-truth framing.
- Pain: Rufus hit 300M users by Q4 2025 with major incremental sales. queries 2.4x
  longer. attributes section now more important than title.
- Framework: 4-step Rufus rewrite (intent map → attributes audit → Q&A bullets →
  review-language reinforcement).

**13. amz-attributes-completer** *(9/10)*
Audits the Seller Central Attributes section against the category template, flags
missing or weak fields and proposes optimal values.
- Pain: The most important SEO field most sellers neglect. most fill under 40% of
  attribute fields.
- Framework: category-template diff + fill priority.

**14. amz-aeo-external-content-plan** *(8/10)*
Plans the external content (Reddit answers, Quora, YouTube reviews, blog posts) that
Rufus's "Researched by AI" section pulls from, so the brand surfaces above its own
listing.
- Pain: Rufus now references external sources before listing content.
- Framework: topic-cluster plan + outreach targets per cluster.

**15. amz-voice-search-listing** *(6/10)*
Optimizes for Alexa and voice-shopping intent (conversational long-tail, "best X for
Y under $Z" phrasing).
- Pain: Voice and conversational queries drive a measurable share of Rufus sessions.
- Framework: intent-phrase generator → bullet rewrite.

## D. Inventory, Logistics, Operations

**16. amz-removal-vs-storage-calculator** *(9/10)*
Decides per-SKU whether to remove, liquidate, dispose, or keep aging inventory based
on storage fees, removal fees, and recoverable resale.
- Pain: 270/365-day auto-removal enforced April 2025.
- Framework: break-even formula + decision matrix.

**17. amz-inbound-placement-optimizer** *(9/10)*
Compares minimal-split vs Amazon-optimized split for an inbound shipment given SKU
dimensions and destination forecast, recommends the lowest landed cost.
- Pain: STA placement fees confuse sellers. choosing wrong option costs $0.30-$1.10
  per unit.
- Framework: per-SKU placement-cost calculator.

**18. amz-restock-forecaster** *(9/10)*
Given 90-day sales data, lead time, and IPI restock limits, outputs a SKU-level
reorder schedule that avoids both stockouts and storage penalties.
- Pain: ASIN-level restock limits reactivated mid-2025.
- Framework: days-of-cover model with safety stock and lead-time variance.

**19. amz-3pl-vs-fba-decision** *(8/10)*
Compares FBA vs 3PL+FBM vs SFP economics per SKU including DD+7 cash-flow effect,
fees, speed badges, and Buy Box impact.
- Pain: Sellers actively reshoring to 3PL+SFP to dodge fees and DD+7 holds.
- Framework: economic model with adjustable assumptions.

**20. amz-sourcing-diversification** *(8/10)*
Builds a supplier-diversification plan moving from China-only to Vietnam, India,
Mexico, light-assembly, with HS-code, lead-time, and tariff trade-offs.
- Pain: 70%+ of Amazon goods are from China. tariff volatility.
- Framework: current-state audit → alt-supplier criteria → migration roadmap.

**21. amz-supplier-negotiation-script** *(8/10)*
Generates a structured negotiation script for Alibaba/Canton suppliers covering unit
price, MOQ, payment terms, lead time, packaging, QC, and tariff sharing.
- Pain: Sellers leave 5-15% margin on the table by negotiating price only.
- Framework: 7-lever bargaining matrix + email template per round.

## E. Advertising and Growth (Beyond the Existing PPC Skills)

**22. amz-tacos-diagnostic** *(9/10)*
Looks at ACoS, TACoS, organic rank, and conversion trends together to diagnose
whether the seller is in the 'ACoS trap' (cutting bids kills organic).
- Pain: Top 2025 PPC pitfall.
- Framework: 4-quadrant ACoS vs TACoS diagnostic + rebalancing plan.

**23. amz-attribution-campaign-planner** *(9/10)*
Plans an Amazon Attribution setup for external-traffic campaigns (TikTok, IG,
Google, YouTube, email) with naming convention, link structure per influencer, and
reporting cadence.
- Pain: Off-Amazon drives 41% of sales but most sellers don't tag traffic properly.
- Framework: channel → tag → KPI mapping.

**24. amz-creator-connections-brief** *(7/10)*
Builds the brief, commission tier, and outreach DM templates for Amazon's Creator
Connections program (and TikTok Shop / Influencer collaborations).
- Pain: Creator Connections expanded in 2025 but sellers don't know how to pitch.
- Framework: product-creator fit scorer + brief template.

**25. amz-sponsored-brands-video** *(8/10)*
Plans a Sponsored Brands Video ad: 30s script, hook structure, on-screen text,
end-card CTA, and version variants for testing.
- Pain: SBV is the highest ROAS ad type in 2025 but sellers lack a script framework.
- Framework: hook → problem → proof → CTA storyboard.

**26. amz-dsp-readiness-audit** *(7/10)*
Assesses whether a brand is ready for Amazon DSP (budget, ASIN coverage, lifecycle
stage), and outputs an audience-and-creative starter plan or a "stay on Sponsored"
recommendation.
- Pain: DSP minimums dropped. many sellers waste money testing without readiness.
- Framework: readiness scorecard + audience tree.

## F. Strategic and Lifecycle

**27. amz-exit-valuation-prep** *(9/10)*
Calculates SDE/EBITDA, applies a 2.5-4x multiplier band based on diversification,
traffic mix, IP, and produces a 12-month pre-exit cleanup roadmap.
- Pain: Aggregator slowdown means sellers must do the prep work themselves now.
- Framework: valuation worksheet + checklist by month.

**28. amz-pricing-war-defense** *(8/10)*
Detects when a competitor or hijacker started a price war on your ASIN and
recommends Buy Box defense moves (coupon, bundle, variation split, walk-away).
- Pain: Frequent forum complaint. auto-reprice race-to-bottom kills margins.
- Framework: signal detection → response ladder.

**29. amz-listing-versioning-tracker** *(7/10)*
Logs every listing change (title, bullets, image, A+, price) with date and impact on
sessions and conversion, so the seller can roll back regressions.
- Pain: Sellers blindly change listings and lose rank without knowing the cause.
- Framework: change-log template + impact-attribution rubric.

**30. amz-customer-question-mining** *(8/10)*
Mines the Customer Questions tab of a listing for unanswered intent, generates
branded answers and feeds the most common questions back into bullets, A+, and
backend keywords.
- Pain: Rufus heavily reads the Q&A section. most sellers leave questions unanswered
  for weeks.
- Framework: question-cluster → answer-and-redeploy loop.
